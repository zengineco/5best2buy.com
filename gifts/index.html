The Gift Guide

A gift card says "I didn't know what you like." A jar of the best hot honey in the country, from a maker they've never heard of, says the opposite. Here's a real, personal gift for everyone on your list — all from small independent makers.

Pick the person, get the shelf. Every link below is a curated list of five genuinely small makers, with honest write-ups and a place to actually buy. Most of it ships anywhere, and none of it is the stuff they could've grabbed at the store themselves.
