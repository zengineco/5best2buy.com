Rice and Peas, and Where to Source It

The peas are actually kidney beans, and the coconut milk is the whole point. Cook the beans first so their starchy liquid tints the rice, then float a whole scotch bonnet on top for perfume without heat, and pull it before it bursts.

Part Two
The Method
  1. Cook the beansSimmer soaked kidney beans in water 45 to 60 minutes until nearly tender, keeping the cooking liquid.
  2. Build the potAdd coconut milk, thyme, scallion, garlic, and the whole scotch bonnet to the beans and their liquid.
  3. Add riceStir in rinsed rice; liquid should sit about an inch above the rice. Bring to a boil.
  4. SteamCover, drop to low, and cook 20 to 25 minutes until liquid is absorbed; fluff and remove the pepper and thyme stems.
