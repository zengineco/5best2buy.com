Chicken Tikka Masala, and Where to Source It

Tikka masala is really two dishes: the grilled tikka and the gravy, and the tikka has to be cooked separately with real char or the whole thing tastes like tomato soup with chicken in it. The one move: cook the marinated chicken on high, direct heat until it colors, then add it to the sauce so the browned edges stay distinct.

Part Two
The Method
  1. MarinateCoat chicken in yogurt, ginger-garlic, garam masala, paprika, and salt. Rest 30 minutes minimum; overnight is better for deeper flavor.
  2. GrillSear the chicken on a screaming-hot pan or grill until charred at the edges and just cooked, about 4 minutes a side. Set aside; it finishes in the sauce.
  3. GravySoften onion, add ginger-garlic and ground spices for a minute, then blended tomatoes. Simmer 20 minutes until thick and jammy.
  4. CombineFold the charred chicken in with any resting juices, stir in cream off the heat, and simmer gently two minutes to marry.
