Taco Salad, and Where to Source It

The whole plate leans on the beef and the chips, so brown good ground beef hard and use real corn tortilla chips instead of stale bagged crumbs. The one technique: brown the meat undisturbed until it crusts before you add the spices, or you steam it gray.

Part Two
The Method
  1. Brown the beefCook the beef over medium-high, pressing it flat and leaving it undisturbed 3-4 minutes to crust before breaking it up.
  2. SeasonPush the meat aside, bloom the chili powder and cumin in the fat 30 seconds, then stir it through with a splash of water.
  3. Warm the beansAdd the beans to the pan and simmer 5 minutes until they slump and pick up the seasoning.
  4. Build the baseSpread shredded iceberg in bowls and scatter tomato, onion, and avocado over it.
  5. Top and finishSpoon the hot beef and beans over the greens, then pile on cheese, salsa, and crushed chips.
