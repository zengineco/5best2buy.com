Cake Pops, and Where to Source It

Cake pops are baked cake crushed and bound with just enough frosting to hold a ball, so do not add so much that they turn greasy. Chill the balls firm before dipping and let the melted chocolate cool slightly, or a hot coating cracks off the cold cake.

Part Two
The Method
  1. Bake the cakeBake a simple vanilla cake at 350F for 22-25 min until a skewer comes out clean, then cool fully.
  2. Crumble and bindCrush the cake to fine crumbs and mix in just 2-3 tbsp frosting until it holds together when pressed.
  3. Roll and chillRoll into 1-inch balls and chill 1 hour until firm so they keep their shape when dipped.
  4. DipMelt the chocolate, dip a stick tip in and push it into each ball, then dip the whole ball and let the excess drip off.
  5. SetStand the pops upright in a block of foam and let the coating set at room temperature.
