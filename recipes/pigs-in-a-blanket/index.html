Straight Answers
Can you make Pigs in a Blanket in an air fryer? — Yes

Yes — bake it in a small pan or dish that fits the basket, at about 25°F below the recipe temperature. Air fryers run hot and fast, so check it early; no flipping needed.

Can you freeze Pigs in a Blanket? — Yes

Yes — freeze it well-wrapped, then reheat or toast straight from frozen. Frost or glaze after thawing.

Can you make Pigs in a Blanket ahead of time? — Yes

Yes — bake it a day or two ahead; it keeps well-wrapped at room temperature and freezes cleanly. Add any frosting or glaze close to serving.

Part Two
The Method
  1. Cut doughUnroll the cold dough and cut into thin triangles, one per sausage.
  2. WrapPat sausages dry, add a dab of mustard, and roll each in a dough triangle from the wide end.
  3. WashArrange seam-down on a lined sheet, brush with beaten egg, and sprinkle with sesame seeds.
  4. BakeBake at 375F for 15-18 minutes until the pastry is deep golden and puffed.
