Profiteroles, and Where to Source It

Profiteroles are cream puffs turned dessert-plate: ice cream inside, warm chocolate poured over. The shell has to be baked crisp and dry, or the ice cream turns it to mush before it reaches the table.

Part Two
The Method
  1. Cook the pasteBoil water, butter and salt, stir in flour, and cook over medium heat 2-3 min until it films the pan bottom.
  2. Beat in eggsCool 5 min, then beat in eggs one at a time to a glossy ribbon.
  3. Pipe and bakePipe 1.5-inch rounds, bake at 425F for 15 min then 350F for 15 min until crisp and dry all through.
  4. Make the sauceWarm chocolate with a splash of cream over low heat, stirring until glossy and pourable.
  5. AssembleSplit cooled puffs, tuck in a small scoop of ice cream, stack on a plate, and pour warm chocolate over just before serving.
