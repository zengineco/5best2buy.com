Home Fries, and Where to Source It

Good waxy or all-purpose potatoes hold their shape here where russets fall apart, so choose accordingly. The whole thing turns on one habit: get the pan hot with real olive oil and resist stirring so a crust can form.

Part Two
The Method
  1. ParcookSteam or microwave the diced potatoes until just tender, then cool. Precooking means they crisp outside without staying raw inside.
  2. Crust the potatoesHeat the oil until shimmering, add the potatoes in a single layer, and leave them to brown before the first stir.
  3. FinishPush the potatoes aside, cook onion and pepper until soft, bloom the paprika, then toss everything together and season.
