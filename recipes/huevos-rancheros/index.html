Huevos Rancheros, and Where to Source It

Good corn tortillas and eggs with real orange yolks make this simple plate; the sauce should taste of toasted dried chile, not just tomato. The technique is crisping the tortilla just enough that it holds up under the sauce without turning to cardboard.

Part Two
The Method
  1. Warm the sauceToast and soak the guajillos, blend into the salsa, then simmer the sauce in a pan over medium heat for 5 minutes until slightly thickened.
  2. Crisp tortillasIn a little oil over medium-high heat, cook each tortilla 30 seconds a side until the edges firm up but the center stays flexible.
  3. Fry the eggsFry the eggs in the same pan until whites set and yolks stay runny, about 2 minutes.
  4. Build the plateSpread beans on the tortillas, set eggs on top, spoon over the warm sauce, and finish with queso fresco and cilantro.
