Hot and Sour Soup, and Where to Source It

The hot comes from white pepper and the sour from black vinegar, so both need to be good and fresh or the soup tastes flat and one-note. The one move: add the vinegar off the heat at the very end, because boiling it cooks the sharp sourness right out.

Part Two
The Method
  1. SimmerBring the broth up with mushrooms, tofu, soy, and white pepper. Let it simmer 10 minutes so the mushrooms soften and season the broth.
  2. ThickenStir in a cornstarch slurry and cook until the soup turns glossy and slightly thick, which lets the egg ribbons float.
  3. Ribbon eggSwirl the soup and pour beaten egg in a thin stream, then stop stirring for a few seconds to let it set into strands.
  4. Sour lastTake the pot off the heat and stir in the black vinegar now, off the boil, so the sourness stays sharp. Finish with scallion.
