The Perfect PB&J, and Where to Source It

Peanut butter on both slices, jelly in the middle. The layer of fat on each side seals the bread against the jelly's moisture, so a PB&J packed in the morning is still intact at lunch instead of a purple soggy mess.

Straight Answers
Can you make The Perfect PB&J in an air fryer? — Not needed

No cooking needed here — this one comes together without heat, so the air fryer sits it out.

Can you freeze The Perfect PB&J? — Yes

Yes — cool it, portion into airtight containers, and freeze; thaw in the fridge and reheat.

Can you make The Perfect PB&J ahead of time? — Partly

The components hold — prep the parts ahead and finish it close to serving for the best texture.

Is The Perfect PB&J gluten-free? — Not as written

Not as written — it contains gluten. Make it gluten-free: swap in gluten-free versions of the wheat items (check the other labels too).

Is The Perfect PB&J vegan? — Yes

Yes — no meat, seafood, dairy, egg, or honey in it as written.
