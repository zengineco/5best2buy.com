Seven Layer Dip, and Where to Source It

Every layer has to be seasoned on its own or the dip tastes flat under all that cheese. Spread the refried beans and guac to the edges so each scoop hits all seven. Build it in a clear dish shortly before serving so the avocado stays green.

Part Two
The Method
  1. BaseSpread the warmed refried beans in an even layer across the bottom of a clear dish.
  2. Guac layerMash avocados with lime and salt, then spread over the beans to the edges.
  3. Creamy layerSpread sour cream over the guac, then spoon salsa across in an even layer.
  4. Top and chillScatter cheese, then diced drained tomato; chill up to 2 hours and serve with chips.
