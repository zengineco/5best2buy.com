Zucchini Fritters, and Where to Source It

Salt the grated zucchini and wring out every drop of water, or the batter is too wet to crisp. Real parmesan and a fresh egg bind the fritters and add savor. Fry in a hot pan with enough oil that the edges go lacy and brown.

Part Two
The Method
  1. DrainGrate zucchini, salt it, wait 10 minutes, then wring it bone-dry in a towel.
  2. BatterMix the dry zucchini with egg, flour, Parmesan, and scallion into a thick batter.
  3. FryDrop spoonfuls into hot oil and flatten; fry 3-4 minutes per side until deep golden.
  4. DrainRest on a rack and salt right away; serve hot with a dollop of sour cream.
