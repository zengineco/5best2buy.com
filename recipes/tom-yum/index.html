Tom Yum Soup, and Where to Source It

Tom yum lives or dies on the broth, so the aromatics have to be bruised and simmered, not boiled to death. Add the lime juice and fish sauce off the heat at the very end — boiling lime juice turns it flat and bitter.

Part Two
The Method
  1. Build the brothSimmer 6 cups water or shrimp stock with lemongrass, galangal, and torn kaffir lime leaves for 10 minutes until fragrant.
  2. SeasonStir in fish sauce and chili paste, then add mushrooms and simmer 3 minutes.
  3. Cook the shrimpAdd shrimp and cook just until pink, about 2 minutes; do not overcook or they turn rubbery.
  4. Finish sourKill the heat, then stir in lime juice and taste; it should hit sour, salty, then hot in that order.
