Sticky Toffee Pudding, and Where to Source It

The dates are what make the sponge dark and fudgy, so soak them in hot water until soft and blend them into the batter. The toffee sauce is cooked separately from butter, brown sugar, and cream, then poured over so it sinks in while the cake is hot.

Part Two
The Method
  1. Soak the datesCover the chopped dates with 1 cup boiling water and a pinch of baking soda, let sit 10 min, then blend to a paste.
  2. Make the batterCream 1/4 cup butter with 3/4 cup brown sugar, beat in the eggs, then fold in the flour and the date paste.
  3. BakeDivide into buttered molds or a pan and bake at 350F for 30-35 min until a skewer comes out clean.
  4. Cook the sauceSimmer the remaining butter, brown sugar, and cream 3-4 min until it thickens and turns glossy.
  5. Soak and servePoke the warm cakes, pour over half the sauce so it soaks in, and serve with the rest on top.
