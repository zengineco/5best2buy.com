Taco Soup, and Where to Source It

Grass-fed beef and real dried beans give the soup a base worth the bowl, and mixing your own taco seasoning skips the cornstarch and MSG of the packet. The one technique: brown the beef hard and leave the fond, then bloom the spices in it, because a scraped-up pot is where the flavor comes from.

Part Two
The Method
  1. Brown the beefCook beef with onion, pepper, and garlic over medium-high until browned, leaving the fond in the pot.
  2. Bloom the spicesStir in the chili powder, cumin, and oregano and cook 30 seconds until fragrant.
  3. SimmerAdd the tomatoes, beans, and broth and simmer 20 minutes so the flavors meld and the broth reduces slightly.
  4. Add the cornStir in the corn in the last 5 minutes so it stays sweet and firm.
  5. ServeLadle into bowls and top with crushed tortilla chips and crumbled Mexican cheese.
