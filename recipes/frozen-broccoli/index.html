How to Cook Frozen Broccoli, and Where to Source It

Don't boil it — that's how you get gray mush. Roast it straight from frozen on a hot sheet pan (or air-fry it): the high, dry heat drives off the ice and browns the edges. Don't thaw it first, and don't crowd the pan, or it steams instead of crisping.

The Method
  1. Heat the panPut a sheet pan in the oven and heat it to 425F — a hot pan is what crisps the bottoms.
  2. TossToss the frozen florets with oil, garlic powder, and salt.
  3. Roast hardSpread them out on the hot pan in one layer and roast about 20 minutes, flipping once, until browned at the edges.
  4. FinishSqueeze lemon over and serve. (Air fryer: 400F about 12 minutes, shaking halfway.)

Straight Answers
Can you make How to Cook Frozen Broccoli in an air fryer? — Yes

Yes — this is exactly what an air fryer does well. Work in a single layer so the air can move, and shake or flip halfway for even crisping. It runs a touch faster than the oven, so check early.

Can you freeze How to Cook Frozen Broccoli? — Not ideal

You can, but the crisp coating softens. Freeze the cooked pieces, then re-crisp them in a hot oven or air fryer — never the microwave.

Can you make How to Cook Frozen Broccoli ahead of time? — Prep ahead

Best fried to order — the crunch fades if it sits. Do the breading or shaping ahead and cook it fresh when you serve.

Is How to Cook Frozen Broccoli gluten-free? — Yes

Yes — nothing in this recipe as written contains gluten. As always, glance at the labels on any packaged items (broth, sauces, chocolate), since brands vary.

Is How to Cook Frozen Broccoli vegan? — Yes

Yes — no meat, seafood, dairy, egg, or honey in it as written.
