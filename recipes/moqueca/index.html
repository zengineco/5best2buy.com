Moqueca, and Where to Source It

Moqueca is a gentle stew, not a boil; the fish should barely poach in the coconut broth so it stays in flakes. Marinate the fish in lime and garlic first, and add it late so it doesn't overcook and fall apart in the pot.

Part Two
The Method
  1. Marinate the fishToss fish with lime juice, garlic, and salt and let it sit 15 minutes.
  2. Layer the potSweat onion and pepper in oil, then layer tomato slices over the top.
  3. Add coconutPour in coconut milk, bring to a gentle simmer, and season.
  4. Poach the fishSlide the fish in, cover, and simmer 8 to 10 minutes until it flakes; finish with cilantro and lime.
