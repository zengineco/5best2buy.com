Arnold Palmer, and Where to Source It

An Arnold Palmer is just iced tea and lemonade, so both halves have to be made right, not from powder. Brew the tea without oversteeping and use fresh lemonade, then blend them at the classic 3 parts tea to 1 part lemonade so the tea still leads.

Straight Answers
Can you make Arnold Palmer ahead of time? — Partly

You can batch the mix and keep it cold, but add the ice, soda, and garnish and shake or stir to order — a made-up drink goes flat and watery if it sits.

Is Arnold Palmer gluten-free? — Yes

Yes — nothing in this recipe as written contains gluten. As always, glance at the labels on any packaged items (broth, sauces, chocolate), since brands vary.

Is Arnold Palmer vegan? — Vegetarian, not vegan

It’s vegetarian but not vegan as written — it has honey. Make it vegan: maple syrup for the honey.

Part Two
The Method
  1. Brew the teaSteep the tea in 4 cups near-boiling water exactly 5 min, remove the bags, and chill.
  2. Make the lemonadeMake a simple syrup, juice the lemons for about 3/4 cup, and mix with 2 cups cold water.
  3. Blend 3 to 1Combine 3 parts tea to 1 part lemonade over ice, taste, and adjust to your liking.
