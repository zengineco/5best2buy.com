Sweet Tea, and Where to Source It

The sugar has to go in while the tea is hot so it dissolves fully instead of settling at the bottom. Steep strong but pull the bags at 5 minutes; over-steeping past that turns the tea bitter and cloudy as it cools.

Part Two
The Method
  1. Boil and steepBoil 4 cups of water, kill the heat, add the bags and a pinch of baking soda, and steep exactly 5 minutes.
  2. Sweeten hotPull the bags and stir the sugar into the hot tea until fully dissolved.
  3. DilutePour the concentrate into a pitcher and top with 4 cups cold water.
  4. ServeChill, then pour over plenty of ice with a slice of lemon.
