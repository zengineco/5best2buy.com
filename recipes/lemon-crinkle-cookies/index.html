Lemon Crinkle Cookies, and Where to Source It

The lemon has to be real zest and juice, not extract, or the cookie tastes like cleaner. The technique is chilling the dough so it scoops firm, then rolling generously in powdered sugar so it splits into the signature crinkle as it spreads.

Part Two
The Method
  1. CreamBeat butter, granulated sugar, and lemon zest 2-3 minutes until pale and fragrant.
  2. MixBeat in the egg and 2 tbsp lemon juice, then add flour, 1/2 tsp baking powder, and 1/4 tsp salt just until combined.
  3. ChillChill the dough 1 hour so it scoops clean and does not spread too fast.
  4. RollScoop tablespoon balls and roll heavily in powdered sugar to coat all over.
  5. BakeBake at 350F for 10-12 minutes until the tops crack and the centers look just set.
