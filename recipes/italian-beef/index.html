Italian Beef Sandwich, and Where to Source It

The beef and the jus are one thing, so a well-marbled roast and a real herb-forward broth make the sandwich. The one technique: chill the cooked roast fully before slicing so you can shave it paper-thin, then bathe those slices back in the hot jus.

Part Two
The Method
  1. Season the roastRub the beef all over with oregano, garlic, salt, and cracked pepper and let it sit an hour, or overnight.
  2. RoastRoast at 300F until it hits 130F internal for medium-rare, about 2 hours, then rest.
  3. Make the jusPour the pan drippings into the broth with more oregano and garlic and simmer 15 minutes into a seasoned jus.
  4. Chill and shaveChill the roast fully, then shave it paper-thin against the grain on a slicer or with a sharp knife.
  5. Build wetWarm the slices in the simmering jus 1 minute, pile onto a roll, dunk the whole sandwich if you want it dipped, and top with giardiniera and peppers.
