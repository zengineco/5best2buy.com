Straight Answers
Can you make Nanaimo Bars in an air fryer? — Not needed

No cooking needed here — this one comes together without heat, so the air fryer sits it out.

Can you freeze Nanaimo Bars? — Not ideal

Not really — the cream, custard, or emulsion turns grainy or splits once thawed. This one is best kept fresh in the fridge.

Can you make Nanaimo Bars ahead of time? — Yes

Yes — it needs the chill time to set, so making it ahead is the whole point.

Is Nanaimo Bars gluten-free? — Not as written

Not as written — it contains gluten. Make it gluten-free: swap in gluten-free versions of the wheat items (check the other labels too).

Is Nanaimo Bars vegan? — Vegetarian, not vegan

It’s vegetarian but not vegan as written — it has dairy and egg. Make it vegan: use plant milk, oil, or a vegan cheese for the dairy, and a flax or aquafaba egg for the egg.

Part Two
The Method
  1. Make the baseMelt butter with cocoa and sugar, stir in egg, graham crumbs, and coconut, and press into an 8x8 pan.
  2. Mix the middleBeat butter, powdered sugar, custard powder, and a little cream to a thick, spreadable icing.
  3. Layer itSpread the icing over the chilled base and chill again until firm.
  4. Glaze and cutPour melted chocolate over, chill, then slice with a hot knife into squares.
