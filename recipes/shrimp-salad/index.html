Shrimp Salad, and Where to Source It

This is a shrimp showcase, so buy the best fresh shrimp you can and poach them gently rather than boiling them tough. The one technique: pull the shrimp from the poaching water the second they turn opaque and shock them in ice so they stay sweet and snappy.

Straight Answers
Can you make Shrimp Salad in an air fryer? — Not needed

It’s a salad — nothing to air-fry once it’s tossed. You can crisp a topping in one, though: croutons, bacon, or chickpeas in a single layer while you build the rest.

Can you freeze Shrimp Salad? — Best fresh

Not really — the vegetables and dressing turn to mush once thawed. Salads are a make-fresh thing.

Can you make Shrimp Salad ahead of time? — Best fresh

Dress it just before serving — the greens wilt and brown once coated. Wash, cut, and chill the components ahead, then toss at the last minute.

Part Two
The Method
  1. Poach the shrimpBring salted water with a lemon half and the Old Bay to a bare simmer, add the shrimp, and cook 2-3 minutes until just opaque.
  2. Shock and peelDrain and plunge the shrimp into ice water, then peel and cut into bite-size pieces.
  3. Make the dressingWhisk the mayo, Dijon, olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper into a light dressing.
  4. Fold togetherFold the shrimp, celery, and herbs into the dressing until just coated.
  5. ChillChill 15 minutes and adjust lemon and salt; serve on greens or in a split roll.
