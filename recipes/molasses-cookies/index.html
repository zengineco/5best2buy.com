Molasses Cookies, and Where to Source It

Molasses cookies live and die on the molasses and the spice blend, so tired ground ginger gives you a dull cookie. The technique that matters is pulling them at 10-11 minutes while the centers still look underdone, so they set up chewy instead of hard.

Part Two
The Method
  1. CreamBeat butter and 1 cup sugar 3 minutes until fluffy, then beat in the molasses and egg.
  2. MixWhisk flour, spices, 2 tsp baking soda, and 1/2 tsp salt, then beat into the wet just until combined.
  3. ChillChill the dough 30 minutes so it firms up enough to scoop cleanly.
  4. RollScoop 1 1/2 tbsp balls and roll each in granulated sugar to coat.
  5. BakeBake at 350F for 10-11 minutes until the tops crackle but centers look soft. They finish setting on the pan.
