Thai Basil Chicken, and Where to Source It

Pad krapow is a five-minute dish once the wok is hot, so mince the garlic and chiles first and have the sauce measured. Holy basil is the whole point — its peppery, clove-like edge is different from sweet Thai basil, and it goes in at the very end.

Part Two
The Method
  1. Pound the baseCrush garlic and Thai chiles into a rough paste with a mortar and pestle.
  2. Fry the eggFry an egg in hot oil until the edges blister and crisp; set aside.
  3. Stir-fryFry the chile-garlic paste 30 seconds, add chicken, and cook hard until browned and slightly crisp.
  4. Sauce and finishAdd fish sauce, soy, and a splash of water; toss, then fold in the basil off the heat.
