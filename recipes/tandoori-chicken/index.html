Tandoori Chicken, and Where to Source It

The yogurt is not just flavor; its acid and enzymes tenderize the meat, so give it at least four hours and overnight is better. Slash the chicken to the bone so the marinade gets in, and roast as hot as your oven goes to mimic the char of a tandoor.

Part Two
The Method
  1. SlashCut deep slashes to the bone in each piece so the marinade reaches the center.
  2. MarinateCoat in the spiced yogurt with garlic, ginger, and lemon. Chill at least 4 hours, ideally overnight.
  3. PreheatSet the oven to 500F with a rack in the top third and a foil-lined tray beneath.
  4. RoastRoast 25 to 30 minutes until charred at the edges and 175F at the bone.
  5. RestRest 5 minutes and finish with a squeeze of lemon.
