Sushi Bowl, and Where to Source It

This is sushi without the rolling, so the rice is where the effort goes: fold in the vinegar mix while the rice is still hot and cool it fast with a fan so the grains stay glossy and separate. Use sashimi-grade fish and slice it just before serving so it does not warm and go soft.

Part Two
The Method
  1. Cook the riceRinse until clear, cook, and let it steam 10 minutes off the heat.
  2. Season the riceWarm vinegar, sugar, and salt until dissolved, fold into the hot rice, and fan it to cool and gloss.
  3. Slice the fishCut the fish against the grain into clean 1/4-inch slices and keep them cold.
  4. Prep toppingsSlice avocado and cucumber and shred the nori just before you build.
  5. AssembleSpread rice in bowls, fan the fish over the top, and add toppings, sesame, and scallion.
