Stuffed Zucchini, and Where to Source It

Zucchini throws water, so the fix is salting the scooped boats and browning the scooped-out flesh before it goes back in. A crust of breadcrumbs and cheese on top gives the soft vegetable something with texture.

Part Two
The Method
  1. Scoop and saltHalve the zucchini lengthwise, scoop out the centers, and salt the boats for 10 minutes to draw out water, then pat dry.
  2. Cook the fillingChop the scooped flesh and saute with onion, garlic, tomatoes, and oregano until the pan is dry, then stir in half the breadcrumbs and parmesan.
  3. Fill and bakeFill the boats, top with the remaining crumbs and cheese, drizzle with oil, and bake at 400F for 20 to 25 minutes until browned.
