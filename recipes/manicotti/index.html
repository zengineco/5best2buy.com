Manicotti, and Where to Source It

A piping bag is the difference between filled manicotti and a torn mess, since spooning into a long tube shreds it; snip the corner of a zip bag if you have none. Boil the tubes only until barely bendable so they survive the stuffing.

Part Two
The Method
  1. Make the sauceCook garlic in oil, add crushed tomatoes, and simmer 20 minutes until thickened.
  2. Boil and coolBoil the tubes until just bendable, about 7 minutes, then drain and cool so you can handle them.
  3. Pipe the fillingBeat ricotta with parmesan, eggs, parsley, and salt, then pipe it into each tube from both ends.
  4. Assemble and bakeLay the tubes in sauce, top with more sauce and mozzarella, and bake covered at 375F for 30 minutes, uncovering the last 10.
