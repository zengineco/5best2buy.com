Philly Cheesesteak, and Where to Source It

Shaved ribeye and a real melting cheese are the whole sandwich, so start with good meat. Freeze the ribeye 30 minutes so you can slice it paper-thin and sear it fast on a hot griddle.

Part Two
The Method
  1. Slice thinHalf-freeze the ribeye, then shave it as thin as you can against the grain.
  2. Griddle onionsCook the onion and pepper on a hot griddle until soft and browned, then push to the side.
  3. Sear meatDrop the shaved beef on the hot surface and chop it with the spatula as it sears, about 3 minutes.
  4. MeltFold the onions back in, lay the provolone over the pile, and let it melt into the meat.
  5. ScoopSet the split roll open-side down over the pile, then scoop the whole thing up into the bread.
