Chicken and Waffles, and Where to Source It

Good eggs and cultured butter make a waffle that crisps outside and stays tender inside, and real maple syrup ties the sweet-and-savory together. The one technique: keep the fried chicken on a rack in a warm oven so it stays crisp while you cook waffles to order.

Part Two
The Method
  1. Fry the chickenDredge chicken in seasoned flour and fry at 350F for 8-10 minutes to 165F internal; hold on a rack in a 200F oven.
  2. Mix the batterWhisk flour, eggs, milk, melted butter, 2 tsp baking powder, and salt into a thick pourable batter; let it rest 10 minutes.
  3. Iron upHeat the waffle iron fully, grease it, and cook each waffle 4-5 minutes until deep golden and no steam escapes.
  4. PlateSet chicken on the waffle and pour warm maple syrup over the whole thing so it soaks the crust.
