Thai Fried Rice, and Where to Source It

Khao pad is lighter than other fried rices — the seasoning is fish sauce and a little soy, not a heavy sauce, so the jasmine rice stays the star. Use cold day-old rice; fresh rice steams and clumps instead of frying into separate grains.

Part Two
The Method
  1. Heat the wokGet the wok hot, add oil, and fry the garlic until just golden.
  2. Cook and scrambleAdd the protein, push it aside, and scramble the eggs in the cleared spot.
  3. Fry the riceAdd the cold rice and fish sauce; toss hard for 3 to 4 minutes until each grain is coated and hot.
  4. FinishFold in scallion and tomato off the heat; serve with lime wedges and cucumber.
