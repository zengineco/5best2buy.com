Ice Cream Sandwich, and Where to Source It

The cookies have to stay soft when frozen or they crack when you bite in, so pull them while still slightly underbaked and a touch bendy. Soften the ice cream just enough to spread, then freeze the whole sandwich hard before cutting.

Part Two
The Method
  1. Make the doughCream butter and sugar, beat in the egg and vanilla, then mix in flour, cocoa, and salt to a soft dough.
  2. Bake softRoll into rounds and bake at 350F for 9-11 min, pulling them while still slightly soft so they stay bendy when frozen.
  3. Soften the ice creamLet the ice cream sit until just spreadable, about 10 min, so it presses without melting.
  4. FillSpread a thick layer of ice cream on one cooled cookie and press a second on top.
  5. FreezeFreeze the sandwiches on a tray at least 2 hours until fully firm before serving.
