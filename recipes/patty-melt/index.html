Patty Melt, and Where to Source It

A patty melt is not a burger, it is a grilled cheese built around a smashed patty, so the bread must griddle in butter to a crisp brown. The onions need real time, 20 minutes low and slow, or they taste raw and the whole thing falls flat.

Part Two
The Method
  1. Caramelize onionsCook sliced onions in butter over medium-low 20 minutes, stirring, until deep brown and jammy.
  2. Smash the pattyForm thin patties and smash on a hot griddle 2 minutes per side to build a brown crust.
  3. Layer the meltBuild rye with Swiss, patty, onions, and more Swiss so the cheese melts against both slices.
  4. Griddle both sidesButter the outside and griddle over medium 3-4 minutes per side until crisp and the cheese runs.
