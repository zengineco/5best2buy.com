Tater Tot Casserole, and Where to Source It

Grass-fed ground beef browned hard gives the hotdish real depth, and a homemade cream-of-mushroom base tastes like food instead of a salt lick. The one technique: bake the tots uncovered on the top rack so they crisp, and never cover the dish or you steam them into gummy paste.

Part Two
The Method
  1. Brown the beefCook ground beef with diced onion over medium-high until well browned and the water has cooked off, about 8 minutes; season with salt and pepper.
  2. Make the sauceIn a separate pan melt butter, saute the mushrooms until browned, stir in flour for 2 minutes, then whisk in milk and simmer to a gravy.
  3. LayerStir the beef and half the cheddar into the sauce, spread in a 9x13 dish, and top with the rest of the cheese.
  4. Top with totsArrange tater tots in a single tight layer over the top so every bite gets one.
  5. BakeBake uncovered at 400F for 35-40 minutes until the tots are deep golden and crisp and the edges bubble.
