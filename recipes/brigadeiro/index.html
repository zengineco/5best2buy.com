Brigadeiro, and Where to Source It

Three ingredients, one technique: cook condensed milk, cocoa, and butter low and slow until the mixture pulls cleanly from the pan bottom. Stop too early and it won't hold a ball; go too far and it turns grainy, so watch for the sheet that lifts off the pan.

Part Two
The Method
  1. Combine and cookCook condensed milk, sifted cocoa, and butter over medium-low, stirring constantly.
  2. Watch the pullAfter 10 to 15 minutes it thickens; it is ready when you drag the spoon and the bottom stays clear for a second.
  3. CoolScrape onto a greased plate and cool fully, at least 1 hour, until firm enough to roll.
  4. Roll and coatGrease your hands, roll into small balls, and roll each through the sprinkles.
