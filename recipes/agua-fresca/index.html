Agua Fresca, and Where to Source It

Agua fresca is fresh fruit blended with water and lightly sweetened, and the two things that matter are straining out the pulp and not oversweetening. Blend the fruit with just enough water to move the blades, then strain and dilute so it's refreshing, not syrupy.

Part Two
The Method
  1. Blend the fruitBlend the fruit with 1 cup water until fully smooth, about 30 sec.
  2. Strain the pulpPour through a fine strainer and press the solids to get a clean, smooth liquid.
  3. Dilute and balanceStir in 3 more cups water, the lime juice, and sugar to taste, then chill and serve over ice.
