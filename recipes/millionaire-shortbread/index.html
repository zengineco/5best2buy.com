Millionaire's Shortbread, and Where to Source It

Three layers, each with its own trick: a crisp shortbread base baked pale, a caramel cooked to 235F soft-ball so it sets sliceable, and tempered chocolate on top that snaps clean. Cool each layer fully before adding the next or they slide.

Part Two
The Method
  1. Bake the baseRub butter, flour, and sugar into a dough, press into a lined pan, and bake at 350F for 20 min until pale gold.
  2. Cook the caramelMelt butter, sugar, and condensed milk together and boil gently to 235F soft-ball, stirring constantly so it does not scorch.
  3. Set the caramelPour the caramel over the cooled base and chill until firm.
  4. Temper the chocolateMelt two-thirds of the chocolate to 115F, then stir in the rest off the heat to bring it to 88F so it sets with a snap.
  5. FinishPour the tempered chocolate over the set caramel, let it firm at room temp, then cut into bars.
