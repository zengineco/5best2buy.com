Straight Answers
Can you make Enchiladas Verdes in an air fryer? — Yes

Yes — bake it in a small pan or dish that fits the basket, at about 25°F below the recipe temperature. Air fryers run hot and fast, so check it early; no flipping needed.

Can you freeze Enchiladas Verdes? — Yes

Yes — it freezes well. Cool it fully, portion into airtight containers, and it keeps a few months; thaw in the fridge and reheat gently.

Can you make Enchiladas Verdes ahead of time? — Yes

Yes — bake it a day or two ahead; it keeps well-wrapped at room temperature and freezes cleanly. Add any frosting or glaze close to serving.

Is Enchiladas Verdes gluten-free? — Yes

Yes — nothing in this recipe as written contains gluten. As always, glance at the labels on any packaged items (broth, sauces, chocolate), since brands vary.

Is Enchiladas Verdes vegan? — No

No — it contains meat, so it isn’t vegetarian or vegan.

Part Two
The Method
  1. Roast the salsaBroil tomatillos, serranos, and half an onion until charred, 8 min, then blend with cilantro and salt until smooth.
  2. Simmer the saucePour the blended salsa into a hot pan with a little oil and simmer 5 min to deepen the flavor and thicken slightly.
  3. Soften tortillasDip each tortilla in warm oil or the warm sauce for a few seconds so it rolls without cracking.
  4. Roll and sauceFill with chicken, roll, and arrange seam-down in a baking dish. Cover completely with the remaining sauce.
  5. BakeBake at 375F for 15 min until bubbling, then top with queso fresco, crema, and onion.
