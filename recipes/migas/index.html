Migas, and Where to Source It

Migas is a way to show off good eggs, so pasture eggs and fried strips of real corn tortilla carry it. The one technique: crisp the tortilla first, then scramble the eggs low and slow so the strips stay crunchy in soft curds.

Part Two
The Method
  1. Crisp the tortillasFry the tortilla strips in a little oil until golden and rigid, then set most aside.
  2. Soften the aromaticsCook onion and jalapeno in the same pan until soft and fragrant.
  3. Scramble lowPour in beaten eggs over low heat and stir slowly for soft, custardy curds.
  4. Fold and finishFold in the crisp strips and cheese just before the eggs set, then top with salsa.
