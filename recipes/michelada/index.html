Michelada, and Where to Source It

A michelada is a savory beer cocktail that lives or dies on fresh lime and a rimmed glass, not on the beer itself. Build the seasoned base in the bottom of the glass first, then pour the cold light beer over it so it mixes as it fills.

Can you make Michelada ahead of time? — Partly

You can batch the mix and keep it cold, but add the ice, soda, and garnish and shake or stir to order — a made-up drink goes flat and watery if it sits.

Part Two
The Method
  1. Rim the glassRub a lime wedge around the rim and roll it in chili salt, then fill the glass with ice.
  2. Build the baseAdd the lime juice, hot sauce, and Worcestershire to the glass and stir.
  3. Top with beerSlowly pour the cold beer over the base so it mixes on its own, and top up as you drink.
