Stuffed Shells, and Where to Source Them

Boil the shells only until pliable, not soft, or they tear when you fill them and collapse in the bake. Season the ricotta aggressively and bind it with an egg so the filling sets instead of running out into the sauce.

Part Two
The Method
  1. Boil the shellsCook the shells until just pliable, about 8 minutes, then drain and lay them out so they do not stick.
  2. Mix the fillingCombine drained ricotta, parmesan, the egg, salt, and pepper until smooth and well seasoned.
  3. Sauce and stuffSpread sauce in a dish. Fill each shell with a heaping spoon of ricotta and set them open-side up.
  4. Top and bakeSpoon sauce over, scatter mozzarella, cover with foil, and bake at 375F for 25 minutes; uncover 10 more until browned.
