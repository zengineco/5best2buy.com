Vanilla Cupcakes, and Where to Source It

A good vanilla cupcake is all about the butter and real vanilla, so do not skimp on either. Cream the butter and sugar until pale and fluffy to build the air that gives a light crumb, and pull them the moment a skewer comes clean so they stay moist.

Straight Answers
Can you make Vanilla Cupcakes in an air fryer? — Yes

Yes — this is exactly what an air fryer does well. Work in a single layer so the air can move, and shake or flip halfway for even crisping. It runs a touch faster than the oven, so check early.

Can you freeze Vanilla Cupcakes? — Yes

Yes — freeze it well-wrapped, then reheat or toast straight from frozen. Frost or glaze after thawing.

Can you make Vanilla Cupcakes ahead of time? — Prep ahead

Best fried to order — the crunch fades if it sits. Do the breading or shaping ahead and cook it fresh when you serve.

Part Two
The Method
  1. CreamBeat 1/2 cup butter with 3/4 cup sugar 3-4 min until pale and fluffy.
  2. CombineBeat in the eggs, then alternate the flour and milk with the vanilla until just smooth.
  3. BakeFill lined tins two-thirds full and bake at 350F for 16-18 min until a skewer comes out clean.
  4. Make buttercreamBeat the remaining softened butter with powdered sugar and vanilla until light and spreadable.
  5. FrostCool the cupcakes fully, then pipe or spread the buttercream on top.
