Sweet Potato Pie, and Where to Source It

Roasting the sweet potatoes instead of boiling drives off water and concentrates the flavor, which is the difference between a rich pie and a watery one. Bake the custard only until the center barely jiggles; it sets as it cools.

Part Two
The Method
  1. Roast the potatoesRoast whole at 400F for 50-60 minutes until collapsing soft, then scoop and mash smooth.
  2. Par-bake the shellLine the crust with parchment and weights, blind-bake at 375F for 15 minutes, then remove weights.
  3. Mix the fillingWhisk mashed potato with eggs, milk, sugar, melted butter, and spices until smooth.
  4. Fill and bakePour into the par-baked shell and bake at 350F for 45-55 minutes.
  5. Check donenessPull it when the edge is set but the center still jiggles slightly; it firms as it cools.
  6. CoolCool completely, at least 2 hours, before slicing.
