Sopes, and Where to Source It

Sopes are a masa dish at heart, so fresh masa harina makes the base tender inside and crisp outside. The one technique: pinch the raised rim while the masa is still warm so it holds the beans and juices without spilling.

Part Two
The Method
  1. Mix the masaKnead masa harina with warm water into a smooth dough that does not crack at the edges.
  2. Griddle the roundsPress thick discs and cook on a comal until the surface sets and the bottom firms up.
  3. Pinch the rimWhile each round is still warm, pinch up a raised edge all the way around to make a boat.
  4. Crisp and loadShallow-fry the bases until crisp, then fill with warm beans, meat, and a crumble of cheese.
