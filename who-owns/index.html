Who Owns Your Food?

Curious who actually owns a grocery brand? Type one in. We'll show you who's behind it — and, if you'd rather buy small, point you to an independent maker who makes something like it.

Why this exists. Most of the grocery store is an illusion of choice — dozens of "different" brands that roll up to the same few owners. That's consolidation, and it's the exact thing Worth The Hunt is built against. Every result here points you to a small, independent maker who still answers to themselves. Ownership is accurate to our knowledge as of June 2026 and changes often — corporate deals move fast, so treat this as a map, not a contract. Not every brand is listed; we add as we go.
