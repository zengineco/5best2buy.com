Privacy Policy

Effective June 2026 · Last updated 29 June 2026

This policy explains what information 5best2buy.com ("Worth The Hunt," "we," "us") collects when you visit, and how it's used. We keep this short and honest.

What we collect

We do not ask you to create an account, and we do not knowingly collect personal information you don't volunteer. If you email us, we receive whatever you send (your email address and message) and use it only to reply.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand, in aggregate, how people use the site (pages visited, general location, device type). Google Analytics sets cookies and may collect usage data as described in Google's Privacy Policy. This data is aggregated and not used to identify you personally.

Advertising

We may display ads served by Google AdSense or similar networks. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings, or opt out of third-party cookies via aboutads.info.

Affiliate links

Some outbound links on this site are affiliate links. When you click one, the merchant or an affiliate network may set a cookie to attribute a resulting purchase to us, so we can earn a commission at no extra cost to you. We do not receive your payment details or personal purchase information — only aggregate confirmation that a sale occurred. See our Affiliate Disclosure.

What we don't do

We do not sell your personal information. We do not build personal profiles of visitors. We do not require any personal data to browse the lists.

Your choices

You can block or delete cookies in your browser settings, and use the advertising opt-outs linked above. Blocking cookies will not stop you from reading the site.

Children

This site is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children under 13.

Changes & contact

We may update this policy; the "last updated" date above will change when we do. Questions about privacy? Email hello@5best2buy.com.
