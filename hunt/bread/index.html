5 Artisan Bread Bakers That Ship

Real bread has four ingredients and a clock — flour, water, salt, wild yeast, and time. Supermarket bread has a label full of dough conditioners and preservatives so it can sit for weeks. The surprising part is that several genuinely small bakeries will ship real naturally-leavened sourdough to your door, either baked fresh or par-baked so you finish it at home. The makers below all ship nationwide. Here's where to buy them.

Published June 2026 · Updated 29 Jun 2026

How this list works. Every baker here is small or independent, bakes naturally-leavened bread with real ingredients, ships nationwide, and earns its spot on merit — nobody pays to be listed. We confirmed each one actually ships direct (most great bakeries don't). We point you to the ones that do.
No.1 · The Gluten-Free Sourdough

Bread SRSLY

San Francisco, CA · gluten-free naturally-leavened

Bread SRSLY makes genuinely gluten-free sourdough that's naturally leavened — no commercial yeast, no gums doing all the work — which is rare and hard to do well. For anyone who's gluten-free and has given up on real bread, this is a revelation: actual sourdough tang and chew without the wheat. It ships nationwide, and it's one of the few small bakeries here built to reach you wherever you are.

Why it isn't on AmazonNaturally-leavened gluten-free bread is a slow, specialized bake with real freshness limits. It ships direct from the bakery because that's the only way to keep it real.

No.2 · The Bake-at-Home Organic

123Dough

Pound Ridge / Brooklyn, NY · certified-organic par-baked

123Dough makes certified-organic, ancient-grain sourdough that's par-baked, so it ships beautifully and you finish the bake at home — meaning the loaf comes out of your own oven hot and crackling. It's a clever solution to the bread-shipping problem, and the ingredients are genuinely clean: organic, heritage grains, naturally leavened. A great option if you want fresh-from-the-oven bread without living near a good bakery.

Bien Cuit

Brooklyn, NY · long-fermented sourdough

Bien Cuit is a celebrated Brooklyn bakery known for its deeply-fermented, dark-crusted sourdough — "bien cuit" means well-baked, and they mean it. Through their Provisions arm they ship their naturally-leavened bread and pastry nationwide. It's bakery-counter quality from a genuine destination bakery, the kind of loaf with real character and a crust worth the name. A treat to ship to yourself or someone who'd appreciate it.
