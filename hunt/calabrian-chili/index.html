Calabrian Chili Worth the Hunt

Calabrian chilies are the secret weapon of great pizza and pasta — a bright, fruity, moderate heat with none of the flat burn of dried flakes. Grown in the far south of Italy and packed whole, crushed, or as a paste (the famous "bomba") in olive oil, they melt into a sauce, dot a pizza, or spike a vinaigrette. A spoonful transforms a dish. Once a jar lives in your fridge, you'll reach for it constantly.
