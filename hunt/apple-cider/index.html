Fresh Apple Cider Worth the Hunt

Real apple cider is just apples — pressed, cloudy, unfiltered, and alive with the flavor of a specific orchard's fruit. The clear "cider" in the grocery jug is usually filtered juice from concentrate, a completely different drink. Fresh-pressed cider is the taste of fall, warm with a cinnamon stick or straight from the fridge, and the base of the best Thanksgiving brines and glazes. The catch is shipping a fresh, unpasteurized-ish liquid — so the best orchards flash-freeze or cold-ship it to arrive intact.

No.1 · The Ships-Anywhere Cider

Kauffman Orchards

Lancaster County, PA · fresh-pressed, frozen for shipping

Kauffman Orchards presses award-winning fresh apple cider and solves the shipping problem the smart way — squeezing, bottling, and then freezing it in a custom insulated box so it arrives in perfect condition anywhere in the country. That means real orchard cider, not concentrate, delivered to your door even if you're nowhere near apple country. For most people, this is the way to get genuinely fresh cider shipped. A standout.

Why buy it directFresh-pressed cider is perishable and hard to ship — which is exactly why the grocery jug is filtered juice from concentrate. An orchard that flash-freezes its own pressing is how you get the real thing at a distance.

Harvard, MA · family orchard, fresh-pressed cider & hard cider

Carlson Orchards is a family-run Massachusetts orchard pressing fresh cider from its own apples, along with a line of hard ciders. New England is the spiritual home of American cider, and Carlson is a well-loved name doing it at real orchard scale. Their online shop makes it easy to get genuine pressed cider from the source — the sweet kind for the kids and the holiday table, or the hard kind for the grown-ups.

Guilford, CT · family orchard since 1871, sweet cider & winery

Bishop's Orchards has grown apples and pressed sweet cider in coastal Connecticut since 1871 — seven generations of the same family — and runs both a farm market and a winery. Their sweet apple cider is the real fresh-pressed article, and the deep history means a level of craft the grocery jug never had. A wonderful, storied New England orchard for genuine cider from a farm that's been perfecting it for 150 years.
