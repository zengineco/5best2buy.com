Reptile Food Worth the Hunt

Reptile nutrition is where pet-keeping gets genuinely technical — a bearded dragon, a crested gecko, and a leopard gecko need three completely different diets, and getting it wrong shows up as metabolic bone disease and a shortened life. The good news: a handful of specialist makers have cracked it, with complete gel diets and gecko formulas that replace the guesswork (and the messy live-feeding, if you want) with real, balanced nutrition. The makers below are the ones experienced keepers and breeders actually trust. Match the diet to your species.
