Tipo 00 Pizza Flour Worth the Hunt

Bread flour and pizza flour are not the same thing. Tipo 00 is milled far finer — talcum-fine — with a protein and gluten structure tuned to stretch thin, hold a long cold ferment, and blister in a screaming-hot oven into that leopard-spotted, chewy Neapolitan crust. Use bread flour and you get bread that happens to be flat; use a real 00 and the dough behaves completely differently under your hands. These are the American-milled 00s worth keeping in the bin.
