Fresh & Raw Dog Food Worth the Hunt

Most kibble is rendered meat-meal and cheap starch, extruded at high heat and shelf-stable for a year — the fast-food of the dog world. Fresh and raw dog food is the opposite: real muscle meat, organs, and produce, gently cooked or kept raw, made in human-grade kitchens and cold-shipped frozen. Dogs eat it like they've been waiting their whole lives for it, and it shows in the coat and the energy. The makers below are the small, serious ones — not the big kibble brands with a "fresh" line.
