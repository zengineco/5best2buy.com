Straight Answers
Fish Food FAQ
What is the best food for a clownfish?

A marine formula built from whole marine proteins — like New Life Spectrum Marine or NorthFin's marine pellets — that mimics a reef fish's wild diet. Look for spirulina and astaxanthin for color, and avoid cheap fish-meal fillers.

How often should I feed my fish?

Once or twice a day, only as much as they finish in one to two minutes. Overfeeding is the number-one cause of fouled water and sick fish — when in doubt, feed less.

Is pellet or flake food better?

Sinking or slow-sinking pellets are usually more nutrient-dense and create less waste than flakes. Flakes suit small surface-feeders; match the food's size and sink rate to how and where your fish eat.

What makes premium fish food worth the extra cost?

Whole-ingredient marine proteins (krill, herring, sardine) instead of terrestrial fillers and cheap fish meal. The result is better color, better health, and less waste polluting your tank — and you often feed less.
